GURU TEGH BAHADUR INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

Guru Tegh Bahadur Institute of Technology is an Indian engineering college affiliated to Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University. It was established in 1999 by the Delhi Sikh Gurudwara Management Committee. GTBIT is a degree level technical institute, approved by the AICTE.
